# 30 Days of Social Posts

Format: one post/day, ~10-15 min to personalise and publish (matches the
"short daily session" constraint). Mix of TikTok/Shorts/YouTube Shorts
(tied to video topics in `../../video-pipeline/NICHE_AND_TOPICS.md`), X
posts, and the community channels named in the LaunchKit (Facebook
allotment/gardening groups, EWB Strathclyde, IChemE student channels).
Swap in your own numbers/photos where marked — don't publish fabricated
specifics; the real build guide and troubleshooting section now have
enough true detail that you shouldn't need placeholders for anything
technical.

Skip a platform on a given day rather than force a low-effort post —
consistency beats volume.

## Rules for all 30 days
- Never state a number you haven't actually measured — pull real figures
  from `garden_log.csv` or the parts list spreadsheet rather than
  estimating.
- Every video day, the caption always includes the plans link — every text
  day, only every 3rd-4th post needs the explicit pitch; the rest build trust.
- If a day's post flops (near-zero engagement), don't force day+1 to be
  bigger — just move to the next item on the list. This is a cadence, not
  a highlight reel.
