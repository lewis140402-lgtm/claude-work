## Winner: Digital product — the solar irrigation build guide

**Justification (under 200 words):** You already own the asset. Everything left — landing page, sales copy, pricing, launch sequence, distribution content — is exactly the work Claude Code can do end-to-end, so your 5-10 hrs/week goes almost entirely into approving and shipping, not producing. It's the only model on the list with genuine first-income odds inside 4-8 weeks: no algorithm gatekeeper (unlike SEO/affiliate or YouTube monetisation, both of which have multi-month lag), no platform fee ceiling (unlike Fiverr/Upwork), and no delivery bottleneck on your time (unlike tutoring, capped at 1:1 hours). Startup cost is £0. HMRC risk is minimal and well-understood — sole trader registration once trading, no VAT concern at this revenue. Freelancing and tutoring score similarly on speed but fail the "% Claude can do" test — they're fundamentally you selling your hours, which doesn't scale and doesn't fit a 5-10hr/week constraint long-term. Video and affiliate models are real but slower-burn; they become the **distribution engine** for the digital product (Phase 2), not the primary income source. Micro-SaaS and template packs are viable *next* products once the pipeline and audience exist — not the day-1 bet.

**Decision: build the solar irrigation guide funnel as the core business; video pipeline is the traffic engine into it.**
