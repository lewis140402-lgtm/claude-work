# Product: The DIY Solar Irrigation Build Guide

## What I need from you before this is sellable

I don't have your actual build content (parts list, wiring diagrams, pump
sizing calculations, photos, lessons learned) — that's your existing asset
and it needs to stay technically accurate, so I haven't invented any of it.
Everything below is the packaging/structure; drop your real material into
the outline and it's ready to sell. This is the one place in Phase 3 where
I need input from you rather than just producing a finished asset.

## Recommended structure (fill in your content under each heading)

1. **Intro** — who this is for, what they'll be able to build by the end,
   what it costs to build (materials budget range), time to build.
2. **Planning your system** — sizing the pump to your water need (litres/day,
   head height, flow rate), sizing the panel to the pump, battery vs
   direct-drive tradeoffs.
3. **Parts list** — exact components you used, with UK/EU sourcing links
   and approximate current prices, plus budget/premium alternatives.
4. **Wiring & assembly** — step-by-step with photos/diagrams, safety notes
   (fusing, waterproofing, earthing where relevant).
5. **Mounting & plumbing** — panel mounting, pipe runs, filtration if needed.
6. **Testing & troubleshooting** — what "working" looks like, common failure
   modes and fixes (the section that justifies the price — this is what
   free YouTube tutorials don't give you).
7. **Running costs & maintenance** — what breaks, when, and what it costs
   to keep it running through a UK winter.
8. **Appendix** — calculation worksheet (reader plugs in their own numbers),
   supplier list, changelog (so it's clear this gets updated).

## Format & tiers

- **Core (£15)**: PDF guide as outlined above.
- **Plus (£29)**: Core + the calculation worksheet as an editable spreadsheet
  + email access for build questions (caps your time — see OPERATIONS.md
  for the weekly time budget for this).
- **Bundle (£45)**: Plus + a 20-minute walkthrough video (can be produced
  with the Phase 2 pipeline's assembly tooling using real footage of your
  own build, not stock clips — screen-record or phone-record once, run it
  through `assemble_video.py`'s subtitle/export step).

Start with Core + Plus only. Add Bundle once you have 5+ sales and know the
guide converts — building the video before validating demand is wasted
effort (see PRICING.md).
