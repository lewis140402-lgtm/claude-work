# Launch Plan

## Week 0 (before launch day) — prep
- [ ] Fill in `PRODUCT.md`'s outline with your actual guide content, export to PDF
- [ ] Build the sizing worksheet as a Google Sheet (view-only link + "make a copy" button)
- [ ] Set up Gumroad product page using `listing-copy/gumroad_listing.md`, set launch pricing from `PRICING.md`
- [ ] Publish `landing-page/index.html` (GitHub Pages is free and works fine for this — or paste content straight into the Gumroad page if you'd rather not host it separately)
- [ ] Line up 3-5 people from your network (engineering course, tutoring students, any solar/allotment contacts) to be the first buyers/reviewers

## Week 1 — direct network launch
- [ ] Day 1: message your 3-5 warm contacts directly, personal note not a broadcast — ask them to buy at launch price and reply with honest feedback
- [ ] Day 2-3: post in 1-2 relevant subreddits/forums using `listing-copy/reddit_forum_post.md` as a base (personalise it — don't copy-paste verbatim)
- [ ] Day 4-7: start daily social posts from `social-posts/30_DAYS_POSTS.md`; first video from the Phase 2 pipeline should go live this week
- [ ] Collect every piece of feedback — DM replies, comments, questions — and log it; this becomes testimonial copy and V2 content

## Week 2 — second push + proof
- [ ] Update landing page with any real testimonials collected in Week 1 (this matters more than any copy tweak)
- [ ] Post in 1-2 more communities, different angle each time (sizing mistakes / cost breakdown / winter survival)
- [ ] Launch pricing ends — raise to standing price (`PRICING.md`), announce the price rise as a reason to buy now in your next 2-3 posts

## Weeks 3-4 — settle into the operating rhythm
- [ ] Move to the steady-state loop in `../OPERATIONS.md`: daily video + weekly repost of guide content, tracked against the week-by-week milestones there
- [ ] Ship Plus-tier email support responses within 48h — this is what earns repeat referrals in tight-knit communities (allotment/homestead/engineering circles all overlap and talk)

## What "launch worked" looks like by end of week 2
10+ sales, at least 2 unsolicited pieces of positive feedback, and a
functioning distribution channel (video pipeline outputting content daily).
If you're well under that, the problem is more likely distribution reach
than the product — see the pivot check in `../OPERATIONS.md` before
changing the product itself.
