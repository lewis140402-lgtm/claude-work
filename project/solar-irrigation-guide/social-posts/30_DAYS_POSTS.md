# 30 Days of Social Posts

Format: one post/day, ~10-15 min to personalise and publish (matches the
"short daily session" constraint). Mix of TikTok/Shorts captions (tied to
Phase 2 video topics), X/LinkedIn text posts, and community engagement.
Swap in your own numbers/photos where marked — don't publish fabricated
specifics.

Platforms: TikTok + YouTube Shorts get the video pipeline output daily
once running (Week 2 onward); X and a relevant Facebook group/subreddit get
a text post most days. Skip a platform on a given day rather than force a
low-effort post — consistency beats volume.

## Rules for all 30 days
- Never state a number you haven't actually measured — "roughly", ranges,
  or "in my case" instead of invented precision.
- Every video day, the caption always includes the guide link — every text
  day, only every 3rd-4th post needs the explicit pitch; the rest build
  trust.
- If a day's post flops (near-zero engagement), don't force day+1 to be
  bigger — just move to the next item on the list. This is a cadence, not
  a highlight reel.
