# Pricing & Launch Offer

## Standing prices

| Tier | Price | Contents |
|---|---|---|
| Core | £15 | PDF build guide |
| Plus | £29 | PDF + editable sizing worksheet + email Q&A (2 questions/buyer cap) |
| Bundle | £45 | Plus + walkthrough video (add once validated) |

## Why these numbers

- £15 is above typical "ebook" pricing (£5-10) because this solves a
  specific expensive mistake (£100+ in wrong parts) — price relative to the
  cost of failure, not to page count.
- £29 Plus tier exists to lift average order value; the spreadsheet costs
  you nothing marginal and email Q&A is capped so it doesn't eat your hours.
- No subscription — one-time purchase matches a one-time build problem.
  Recurring pricing would need recurring value (community, updates feed,
  new designs) you don't have bandwidth to run yet.

## Launch offer (first 2 weeks only)

- **£10 Core / £22 Plus**, clearly marked "launch price, rises to £15/£29 on
  [date]". Creates urgency without discounting forever, and gives you real
  conversion data at a price point you can compare against standing price
  later.
- First 10 buyers: DM them post-purchase asking for a testimonial/review in
  exchange for the Bundle video tier free when it ships. This is how you
  get review copy for the landing page without paying for it.

## Money-in-hand math

- 10 sales at launch pricing (mixed Core/Plus, ~£16 avg) = £160.
- Break-even on your time isn't the bar here — the guide already exists.
  The bar is: does organic + network distribution get you to 10-15 sales/
  month by month 2? If yes, this is your income engine; if no by month 3,
  see the pivot metric in `../OPERATIONS.md`.
